ChatGPt has continued to remain a hot topic on social media since its launch in 2020!

Looking at how everybody had something to say about ChatGPT, I decided to analyze ChatGPT-related tweets and visualize them to find trends and acitvities that coincide with real-life events.

In this project, I performed Exploratory Data Analysis and developed several visualizations using MatplotLib, Seaborn, Plotly and Dash.

Designed inspired by BootstrapMade, improved by yours truly.
